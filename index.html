About Us

We're a group delivering news, reviews and interviews on feminist movies to people across the country!

Like our balloons, our main purpose is to uplift our readers!

Our speciality is on movies and their creators that focus on representation of women, whether that be within the movie itself, or through its production. Our group is passionate about bringing cutting-edge journalism towards an under-explored genre of movies.

We found that most articles about women's representation is quite negative, so we pride ourselves with focusing on the positive aspects of women's representation within films and the media industry, showing that improvements are always being made when it comes to gender equality.

Possibly the most anticipated movie of 2023. It explores the delicate, blossoming and intertwining web of life.


Two women from opposing backgrounds.

Shen, played and mastered by the talented actress Leeann Lam, is the shy but kind-hearted daughter of a million-dollar businessman. Kajal is played by the up-and-coming actress Anishka Addepalli and is a free-spirited artist living in the suburbs.


Two women with seemingly no connection, until an unexpected event forces them to cross paths. Throwing these two lives together unravels a series of events which reveals the true depth of our connectivity.

And to all the women out there, keep going, you've got this!

- Amirah Awan


Behind the scenes.

Watching the magic behind the scenes was an immense moment for me. I was struck by the electric atmosphere and it opened my eyes to the amazing attention to detail. Everything from the incredible set design to the masterful camera work really showcases the amount of thought given to this movie. Seeing the distinct sections of the production crew work so cohesively in such a friendly environment was so different from other movie sets I’ve seen in the past, and was nothing short of inspiring.

Big dreams and a way to get there.

Being able to sit down with the director, Amirah Awan, and have a heart-to-heart conversation made this movie that much more amazing. She expressed her goal to create more diversity within the media industry, starting with giving women a place to express their creativity in a movie of this size. Talking to some of the crew and cast, it was evident that Amirah’s love towards the production extends throughout the entire team, which was fantastic to see.

Another part of the interview revolved around Amirah wanting to inspire the next generation of filmmakers. With the amount of women in the film industry decreasing over the last year (24% this year, from 25%), she wants to help counteract this by encouraging people to follow their passion of filmmaking.


Our Lives: The future of film.

Amirah Awan is truly the master magician and their magic is evident in her presentation of relationships in a completely fresh way. Instead of just focusing on the queer relationship at hand, it's integrated seamlessly into an engaging plot, which is a huge step towards the normalisation of queer relationships in film.

Amirah’s passion for creating a more inclusive and equal workplace has proved to be fruitful. The all-women cast and crew is truly an amazing step toward equality in the media industry and this just adds to the importance and wonder of the film. Her determination to provide women with equal opportunities is inspiring and something we all wish to see more of. Her energy towards Our Lives was uplifting and, after leaving the interview, it was comforting knowing that the media industry would be headed with people like her - changing the world for the better.


Our Lives will be shown on theatres across the UK and abroad on Janurary 21st 2023.

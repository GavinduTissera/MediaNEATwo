Possibly the most anticipated movie of 2023, Our lives, is a must-see film that explores the interconectedness of us as people, and the unexpected relationships that can blossom within them. The movie begins with two women from vastly different backgrounds. Shen, played by the talented actress Leeann Lam, is the shy but kind-hearted daughter of a million-dollar businessman, while Kajal (played by the up-and-coming actress Anishka Addepalli) is a free-spirited artist living in the suburbs. The women seem to have no connection, until an unexpected event forces them to cross paths. This event makes them discover that their lives are more intertwined than they ever thought possible, which kickstarts a wild journey through time, confronting the obstacles that threaten to tear them apart.

We put a huge focus on being realistic instead of showing relationships to be a utopia, we focus not only on the ups, but also the downs of their new lives together.

- Amirah Awan

I couldn't have been more excited to see the magic behind the movie on set, and I was immediately struck with the electric atmostphere that filled the entire studio. Everyone from the cast to the crew was incredibly friendly. What really astounded me was the amazing attention to detail maintained by Amirah. The set design was impeccable, and it was faciniating to see the crew work together to capture the perfect shot.
